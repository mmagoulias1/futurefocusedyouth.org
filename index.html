Our Impact

Through our innovative financial literacy programs, we've empowered countless students with essential money management skills. Our hands-on approach combines real-world experience with expert guidance, creating lasting impact in our community.

Each workshop and event is carefully designed to build confidence and understanding in personal finance, preparing the next generation for financial success.

Our Mission

We believe that financial literacy is a fundamental right. Our mission is to provide accessible financial education that empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their future.

Meet Our Founder

Nick Laslo, the founder of Future Focused Youth, is dedicated to empowering the next generation with essential financial literacy skills. Through his innovative approach and passion for education, he has developed comprehensive programs that combine real-world experience with expert guidance.

With a strong background in finance and education, Nick has created a platform that makes financial literacy accessible and engaging for young people. His vision has helped shape the future of countless students, providing them with the tools and knowledge they need for financial success.

Recognizing Our Co-Founder

Theo Moss

Co-Founder & Director of Community Programs

Theo Moss has been instrumental in shaping the vision and impact of Future Focused Youth. With a passion for community empowerment and a background in youth development, Theo has led the creation of innovative programs that reach and inspire young people across the city.

Their dedication to financial literacy and inclusive education continues to drive our mission forward, ensuring every voice is heard and every opportunity is accessible.
